Ouroboros

The Island City of Cannibals

work home

Ouroboros is an island city inhabited by giant 7-foot-tall cannibal aliens capable of hyper intelligence. They are extremley empethetic and lack the self preservation humans inhibit so they voluntarily sacrifice themselves in order to provide food, organs, leather, bones, etc. to the living. They use little wood, animals- or living things to in general- to enhance their city and quality of life. They are often misinterperted as 'savage' and 'barbaric' for their naturally inclined practice of canniblaism, so to combat these misconceptions here is a gallery of journalistic illustrations presenting everyday life for citizens of Ouroboros.
