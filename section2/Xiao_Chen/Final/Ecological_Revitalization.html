Ecological Revitalization

Among many encounters with ecology in landscape, Guiyu is definitely the most extreme and striking one for me.

E-waste dismantling was the only source of income of the local people and they were ready to expel anyone who tried to suppress it, even though they knew that the pollution was killing them.

Thatâ€™s why we used the waste to build landform without completely hauling the waste away. The topography are designed in a way where the water can drain efficiently. And this drainage system is helped by rock patches, rough river beds, dams and wetlands to purify the liquid waste.

Through these ecological treatments, water, soil and vegetation can be gradually restored to sustain healthy live of local people.

Time: 2015

Project Location:Guiyu, Guangdong, China
