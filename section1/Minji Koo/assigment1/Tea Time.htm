About Tea

Teas are wooden or plastic stand used to prop up golf balls in a golf course. They are designed with two ends. One end is sharp and pointed, meant to be easily inserted into sod, while the other is circular and shaped like a cup in order to facilitate the spherical form of a golf ball.
