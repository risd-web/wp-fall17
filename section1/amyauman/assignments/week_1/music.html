The Music

Telluride Bluegrass Festival features four full days of fantastic bluegrass music. The lineup changes every year, but you can bet on several oft-returning acts, including Punch Brothers and Yonder Mountain String Band. The Telluride House Band blows it up every year, with the bluegrass greats all on one stage: Bela Fleck, Jerry Douglas, Stuart Duncan, Sam Bush, Edgar Meyer and Bryan Sutton. If that's not enough, attend one of the Nightgrass shows after hours, or catch a free workshop at the Elk Park stage.
