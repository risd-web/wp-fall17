NO PARKING IN A BIKE LANE

§ 31-21-4(a)(16) No person shall stop, stand, or park a vehicle, except when necessary to avoid conflict with other traffic or in compliance with law or the directions of a police officer or traffic control device, in any of the following places:

  On any bicycle lane, trail or path.
